Services

Our platform is designed to assist client in movement of goods from one point to the other with minimum effort and superb convenience. We provide solution to clients who are looking for the most affordable rate to transport cargo, hire construction vehicles and passenger vehicles for any of the following purposes:

Client Deliveries

We offer a superb solution for deliveries of goods safely to your clients. We organize same day deliveries where picks are done in the morning and drops done in the afternoons. You can also place an order for express deliveries to be done within 1 - 2 hrs at a premium rate.

Cross Border Transport

Our Transporters offer you the bests level of services for all your transport needs thought east and central Africa. Our trucks and trailers fleet have experience of handling jobs within the region and will be at hand to assist with border clearance and document processing.

Construction machinery

Our platform gives you access to various construction vehicles like Tipper Trucks, Compactors, Wheel Loaders, Excavators & Breakers, Graders, Bulldozers, Cranes and Forklifts.

Courier

We handle courier deliveries within Nairobi using motor bikes and outside Nairobi using vans, pickups and trucks.

Business Supplies

Our services provide a solution for your business supplies from major towns. We provide transport services to hardwares, motor spare shops, wholesale supplies, supermarkets, general shops, construction supplies and electrical shops.

House Moving

Our team of professional transporters are always at hand to offer house moving services from one estate to other neighborhoods, major towns,cities and rural homes within the country.

Office Relocation

We plan and organize together with your company to offer smooth office relocation per your schedule ensuring that business operations are not interrupted.

Farm Produce Transport

Farmers frequently contact us for transportation of farm produce from their farms to the various market destinations. We are now able to offer this service with ease and convenience.

Tour Vans & Buses

Book your family, safari group or company passenger transport using Tour Vans (8 Pax), Landcruisers (8 Pax) and Minibuses (22 Pax).
