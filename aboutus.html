About Trevy's Edge

Treaville Edge Trucks has been in operation since 2015 and is the fastest growing transport company in Kenya. We aim to provide our clients with the most affordable rates for transporting goods within the country and across the neighbouring countries. Our network, field operations and planning will be able to offer our clients upto 40% discounts to most destinations. The client sets the price and our transporters proceed to bid for the jobs.

We offer solutions related to Transport, Passenger & Tour Vehicles and Construction Vehicles. We will be able to offer great discounts for services in these 3 transport categories that will be beneficial to clients and service providers.

We will connect you to our transport professionals who will provide services with utmost courtesy. Treaville Edge makes it fast, simple and cost-effective to find the best transporters in the industry offering best discounted prices from various destinations in the country.

We pride ourselves in being an innovative transport company that connects customers in need of affordable transport to bidders willing to provide services at negotiated prices. Transporters in turn benefit by getting guaranteed cargo on all their trips to various destinations.
