Services

Our platform is designed to assist client in movement of goods from one point to the other with minimum effort and superb convenience. We provide solution to clients who are looking for the most affordable rate to transport cargo, hire construction vehicles and passenger vehicles for any of the following purposes:

RATE CALCULATOR

Use the provided rate calculator on Book Job tab to find out minimum price and most ideal rate for the job. This will help the client to quote a rate that would be attractive to several service providers.

BOOK A DELIVERY/JOB

You can book a delivery or job by filling the form provided in Book service form, calling us to provide details or sending an email.

DASHBOARD

All booked deliveries or jobs will appear on transporters profile under Jobs Dashboard and Client jobs Dashboard. Interested transporter will open job and click on apply button. The job will be automatically moved from the main dashboard to transporter.

PAYMENT PROCESS

Once a job has been picked by a service provider, Client will be requested to make full payment via Mpesa or Transfer to our bank account. Payment is released to the service provider upon confirmation of safe delivery of goods.

PICK UP & DELIVERY

Upon payment the client details are sent to the transporter to provide details of the pick up location, most ideal time that the job can be done. The service provider confirms and avails vehicle at the agreed place and location.

TRACK YOUR DRIVER

Client provides a delivery note with a list of all goods which the driver has to sign and confirm receipt. Track your driver in real time. We encourage clients to avail one of their staff to accompany the goods as well.
