How Treaville Edge will work for you.

Treaville edge provides you with a platform where you can register with us and pick jobs from any location within the country. This creates a good flow of business for our partners. A truck driver can pick jobs on their way to a pick or on return from a drop off destination. A combined solution for several jobs along a particular route can be agreed upon with the clients to provide an affordable solution for their transport jobs. Most vehicle owners will pick a job that is convenient to their location and work schedule.

We will connect drivers and truck owners to more jobs enroute to various destinations in a transparent, efficient and secure way. Clients will place job orders with us, then our partners will be able to log on to the Jobs Dashboard Tab to pick the jobs that can be conveniently done to earn extra cash. When you click on Apply button, you will automatically receive a message giving you details of the job. The client will be requested to make full payment for the job to Treaville Edge. Once payment is received, the driver gets a dispatch message with the clients contact and location. Once the job is completed and client confirms, you will receive payment on the same day.

Transporter Benefits

Return cargo at your nearest point

The driver gets to load his vehicle at a location that is nearby hence does not incur extra movement cost. You will always get return cargo load close to your drop off point.

High Volume of Jobs

Our Platform will deliver more jobs than any other platform. This way drivers get to choose the best job to pick for the day.

Full trip Cargo Load

It’s now possible for you to get loads for 2 way journey hence charge clients favourable rates. A transporter can also pick 2-3 cargo loads from one destination to the other or enroute.

Reduced Downtime

Jobs from this platform ought to keep a driver well engaged throughout the month. Log on to check new jobs that are being posted by clients.

Combined Solution

A driver can offer a variety of solution for clients in the same route. This way the cost of transport becomes affordable to clients.

Cargo at your Convenience

Those times that you are in a location and you don’t know where to get cargo to transport are sorted. You can pick jobs enroute to your other pick up point or within your current location.

Quick Jobs

Registration Process

Click on sign up button at the top right of our website or at the bottom of this page. Then scan and attach the following documents to our transporters’ sign up form that will pop up:

  • Vehicle Logbook
  • Vehicle Insurance Certificate
  • Owner’s ID / Company Reg Copy
  • Driver’s ID Copy
  • Driver’s Licence

Transporter Payment:

We submit payments to owners at the end of every day. Once a job has been confirmed as completed, we begin the payment process to the transporter. Payment is sent to Mpesa number or Bank account number that is provided during registration / sign up.

Commissions:

Treaville Edge charges 10% commission for every job that is done through our platform. We do not have any other additional or hidden charge.
